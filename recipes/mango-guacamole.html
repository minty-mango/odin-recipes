Mango Guacamole

Desciption

A twist on traditional guacamole, with a sweet mango tang! Enjoy with chips!

Ingredients

  • 3 large avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1 small red onion, chopped
  • 1 mango - peeled, pitted, and chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. Mash avocados in a medium bowl to a lumpy consistency. Mix onion, mango, lime juice, garlic, salt, and cilantro into avocado.
